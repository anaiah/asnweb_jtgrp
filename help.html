OnRoute App - User Guide

Welcome to the ASN-OnRoute User Guide

This guide will help you get started and make the most of ASN-OnRoute App.

Getting Started

Requirements:

  • Android / iPhone / Laptop
  • Chrome Browser
  • WiFi / Internet Connection

You can Use either Android/Iphone/Laptop to open the mobile app and we use Chrome for opening the app. Please do not use Internet Explorer, Opera or any browser -- they are not suitable for the app's standard and operability.

[Login]

This is to enable you to use the app, especially for Time-in and Initial Parcel Quantity input. USE / DO THIS IN THE MORNING AFTER SCANNING PARCELS!

IMPORTANT Reminders:

  • The first time you use the fone, please be inside the Hub where you get your parcels. This will register the Hub's location.

    For your next transaction -- if you will try to login and you are not inside the Hub or you are more than 20meters far, it will not let you use the system, you must be inside the Hub where you used your phone for the first time.

  • Please refrain from borrowing other's fone to Login or to transact remittance, your phone is dedicated to you, and other users can't login.
  • The system is available to use frm 6am to 11pm. If you use the app beyond 11pm you will be denied.

Watch the video below for step-by-step instruction on how to use the Mobile App.

Step 1: [Go to the site address]

Type in the URL Address of browser https://asianowapp.com.ph/jtx

Step 2: [Start Entry]

As you see in the video, immediately after you logged, the "Start Entry Modal" will open automatically.

Open your Mobile app as soon as you finished scanning your parcels. You will input the Quantity that you will deliver.

After encoding the quantity, it will be saved in our Database and the system will create your TIME-IN for the day.

If you wish to logout press the Menu icon on top left (3 dash lines), then select LOGOUT

For multiple waves of Delivery, you can use the START REMITTANCE module multiple times until you REMIT your cash.

Now you are ready to Deliver your Parcels!

Step 3: [Remmitance Entry]

Important Reminders when putting values in Remittance form.

  • You can correct, change the value of "Parcel(s) from Hub", the value should be the number of parcels scanned.
  • For the "Actual Parcels Delivered", the value should be the Actual Parcels that you Delivered.
  • The COD amount (left of Sprinter App) Should be copied in "Hub Amount"
  • The COD Remittance Amount (right of Sprinter App) should be copied in "Actual Amount Remittance"
  • The Screenshot of Your Sprinter App (see Fig.2) should be uploaded on the last part of the Form (see video below how) as a "receipt" of your work.

Watch the video below for step-by-step instruction on how to use the Remittance Entry.

Your Daily Transaction is Done after Saving, You can now LOGOUT.

Troubleshooting

[Can't Connect, Can't Open the Site]

Please check for the following:

  • Check Internet connection
  • Check the Address on the URL of browser should be "https://asianowapp.com/jtx"
  • Check the Email address that you entered, maybe it's different from the one registered in Database. ask your Coordinator for Help.
